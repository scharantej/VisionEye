Welcome to the Eye-Contact Association

The Eye-Contact Association is a non-profit organization that provides support and resources to blind and visually impaired people in Israel. We are dedicated to empowering our members to live full and independent lives.

We offer a variety of programs and services, including:

  • Job training and placement
  • Assistive technology training
  • Independent living skills training
  • Social and recreational activities

We also advocate for the rights of blind and visually impaired people in Israel. We work to ensure that our members have equal access to education, employment, and all other aspects of society.
