About Us

The Eye-Contact Association is a non-profit organization that provides support and resources to blind and visually impaired people in Israel. We are dedicated to empowering our members to live full and independent lives.

We were founded in 1992 by a group of blind and visually impaired Israelis. We have since grown to become one of the leading organizations in Israel that provides services to blind and visually impaired people.

Our mission is to provide blind and visually impaired people in Israel with the tools and resources they need to live full and independent lives. We believe that everyone, regardless of their vision, deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe that blind and visually impaired people can achieve anything they set their minds to. We provide them with the tools and resources they need to succeed.
  • Independence: We believe that blind and visually impaired people should be able to live their lives independently. We provide them with the skills and support they need to do so.
  • Inclusion: We believe that blind and visually impaired people should be fully included in all aspects of society. We advocate for their rights and work to ensure that they have equal access to education, employment, and all other aspects of society.

Our Team

The Eye-Contact Association is staffed by a team of dedicated professionals who are committed to providing the best possible services to our members. Our team includes:

  • Social workers
  • Rehabilitation counselors
  • Job trainers
  • Assistive technology specialists
  • Administrators

We are also supported by a team of volunteers who donate their time and energy to help us achieve our mission.
